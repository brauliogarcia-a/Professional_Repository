Overview

Purpose

The purpose of the site is to have another official channel for the company. With the site we expect that clients trust our brand, and they can understand what we do and how we can help their business. We will use the site as another advertising channel.

Audience

Our audiences are all small and big companies that have webistes. Our audicenes have the need to understand what their clients are doing on their websites, and through analysis take data driven decisions. The main audience are companies that have dedicated teams that maintain the analytics infrastructure and have a marketing team. Our second audience are companies that are lookign for recommendations and light web analysis due to the lack of proper teams.
